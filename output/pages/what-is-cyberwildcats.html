What is Cyberwildcats?

This presentation by the coach from August 2022 explains what Cyberwildcats is. All Wilmington University students are welcome to join the club. All Wilmington University alumni are equally invited to apply. Those who aren't students or alumni are welcome to apply if they have a sponsor from Wilmington University. WilmU students and alumni are not required to provide a sponsor.
